About Me

My Web Design Services


My name is Lukasz Adam. I code, I design and I optimise pages to increase their traffic. I solve problems, to help others create their vision online. It doesn't matter, if you need something new or if you need help with something you already created, I am sure I can help, to find the best way to achieve your goal.

One specialty that you can expect from me, is that I work fast, efficiently and that I create my User Interfaces for websites with lots of illustrations and colors. The website needs to breath, that is why I make sure to add enough White Space to it.

You know the feeling when you land on a website, and think to yourself: “Wow, this is a clean modern approach”. From my point of view, this is the best way to present your product or service.

I love to learn new languages and that is what brought me to Barcelona. Even I wasn’t born in this great city, I never felt more home like in Barcelona. The first time I’ve visited Park Güell or the Sagrada Familia, I was impressed by all the beautiful colors. Catalonia's capital is a city is a full of life. As a Freelancer, after designing and coding a website, there is nothing more relaxing than enjoying the view from Barcelonetas Beach.

There are lots of great Designers, Entrepreneurs and Startups emerging from Barcelona, that is why it is such a great place to work as a Freelancer. With its 1.6 million people, there is always a new project to begin with.

I love to travel and that’s why I spend my time on several places all over the world. I love to work with people from all over the world and I love to inspire them and teach new things to them as well as learning new things for myself.

If you like to know more about me, please feel free to contact me anytime you like. Just use my Email or my Social Media channels.
